Angel Names

Male

  1. ABADDON:Greek name derived from the Hebrew abaddown,meaning 'destruction ruination.' In the New Testament bible, this is the name of the place of destruction.
  2. AINGERU:Besque from Latin Angelus, meaning "angel,messanger."
  3. ANGE:French name meaning"angel,messanger."Compare with feminine Ange"
  4. ANGELUS:Latin name derived from the word angelious, meaning angelic
  5. ANJELO:Variant spelling of Italian Angelus,meaning"angel, messanger"

Females

  1. AINGEAL:Irish Gaelic form of Latin Angela,meaning"angel,messanger"
  2. ANDELA:Czech form of Latin Angela, meaning"angel,messanger"
  3. ANDJELA:Serbian form of Latin Angela,meaning"angel"
  4. ANGE:English short form of Latin Angela,meaning"messanger"
  5. ANGELA:Femine form of Latin Angelus,meaning"Angelic Messanger"
